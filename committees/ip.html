International Press

Covering AMIMUN 2026

About International Press

The press serves as the conscience and lens of the conference. Delegates in this committee will assume roles of journalists, photojournalists, and editors, documenting developments, conducting interviews, and producing daily newsletters. IP fosters skills in investigative reporting, political analysis, and narrative-building.

This committee is perfect for delegates interested in journalism, media studies, or those who want to experience MUN from a different perspective. You'll develop skills in news writing, interviewing, photography, and digital media production while covering the most newsworthy events of the conference.

Roles and Responsibilities

Print Journalists

Write articles, conduct interviews, and cover committee sessions for the daily conference newspaper.

Photojournalists

Capture key moments throughout the conference and provide visual content for publications.

Broadcast Journalists

Produce video content, conduct on-camera interviews, and create news packages.

Social Media Team

Manage conference social media accounts and provide real-time updates.

Committee Details

Committee Type: Offline

Delegation: Individual Role (No country assignment)

Positions Available: Reporters, Editors, Photographers, Videographers
