Welcome to AMIMUN '26

“The price of excellence is discipline. The cost of mediocrity is disappointment.” – William Arthur Ward.

This reminder reflects the essence of AMIMUN’26. True excellence in governance, diplomacy, or personal growth rests on discipline: the discipline to learn with humility, act with integrity, and persevere with resilience. Without it, ambition falters, and what remains is mediocrity, bringing only disappointment.

It is in this spirit that I, Debanjan Dasgupta, Secretary-General of AMIMUN’26, welcome you all to the 15th edition of the Amity International Model United Nations. From January 16th to 18th, 2026, at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh, I envision AMIMUN’26 as a platform where young minds unite in diplomacy, dialogue, and discovery anchored in discipline and driven toward excellence.

Virtus Per Disciplinam-“Excellence through Discipline”, the theme of this year, reminds us that true leadership is not merely defined by rhetoric or ambition, but by the unwavering consistency to strive for meaningful impact, the resilience to overcome adversity, and the steadfast discipline to remain anchored in our principles. Discipline serves as the compass that directs us toward responsibility, clarity, and advancement at a time when divides and diversions sometimes impair judgment. We foster excellence in ourselves and the communities we serve by practicing this discipline of thinking, action, and purpose.

At AMIMUN, we believe that discipline is the cornerstone of true leadership, shaping individuals who are principled, steadfast, and prepared to confront the complexities of our world. By guiding delegates to think with discernment, listen earnestly, and act with integrity, this platform transforms every dialogue into an experience that cultivates perseverance, patience, and meaningful growth. This edition is based on the direction and unwavering support of our Founder President,Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan Sir Chancellor Atul Chauhan Sir, Vice Chancellor Prof. (Dr.) Balvinder Shukla Ma'am, Additional Pro-Vice Chancellor Sanjeev Bansal Sir, Chairperson Dr. Laxmi Ahuja Ma'am, and Co-Chairperson Dr. Sumitra Singh Ma'am. For this, I am incredibly grateful.

Come celebrate with us at AMIMUN’26 as we continue a tradition of excellence, molded not by accident but by the discipline that defines greatness, inspires purpose, and drives leadership.

Warm regards,

Debanjan Dasgupta
Secretary General

What You'll Learn at AMIMUN

Global Awareness

Develop a deeper understanding of international relations, global issues, and the workings of the United Nations.

Public Speaking

Enhance your ability to articulate thoughts clearly and persuasively in front of an audience.

Diplomacy

Learn the art of negotiation, compromise, and building consensus among diverse perspectives.

Critical Thinking

Analyze complex issues, evaluate solutions, and think on your feet during debates.

Teamwork

Collaborate with peers to draft resolutions and work towards common goals.

Leadership

Develop leadership skills by taking initiative and guiding your committee towards solutions.
