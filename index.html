Welcome to AMIMUN '26

"From debate to dialogue, from words to actions. Diplomacy is about the interest of the people not the power of the state."

Welcome to the 14th edition of the Amity International Model United Nations Conference (AMIMUN 2026), where diplomacy meets innovation and global challenges find collaborative solutions. Set against the backdrop of an increasingly interconnected world, AMIMUN 2026 brings together young leaders, thinkers, and change-makers to engage in meaningful dialogue, foster cross-cultural understanding, and craft resolutions to the most pressing issues of our time.

This year's conference, themed "Navigating Global Challenges: Building Bridges for a Sustainable Future," challenges delegates to think critically about the complex interplay between technology, sustainability, and international cooperation. As we gather in the spirit of diplomacy, we aim to create an inclusive platform that not only simulates the United Nations but also inspires actionable ideas that transcend the committee room.

Join us for an unforgettable experience of debate, diplomacy, and personal growth. Whether you're a seasoned MUN participant or new to Model UN, AMIMUN 2026 promises to be a transformative journey that will expand your horizons and connect you with like-minded individuals from around the world.

What You'll Learn at AMIMUN

Global Awareness

Develop a deeper understanding of international relations, global issues, and the workings of the United Nations.

Public Speaking

Enhance your ability to articulate thoughts clearly and persuasively in front of an audience.

Diplomacy

Learn the art of negotiation, compromise, and building consensus among diverse perspectives.

Critical Thinking

Analyze complex issues, evaluate solutions, and think on your feet during debates.

Teamwork

Collaborate with peers to draft resolutions and work towards common goals.

Leadership

Develop leadership skills by taking initiative and guiding your committee towards solutions.
