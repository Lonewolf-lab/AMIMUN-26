Meet Our Advisory Board

Guiding AMIMUN 2026 with wisdom, experience, and vision

Gauri Sareen

Chief Advisor

Dear AMIMUN Family,

As I sit down to write this message, my heart is filled with emotions that words can barely capture. The journey I have had with AMIMUN is not just a chapter in my life; it is a saga of growth, belonging, and unforgettable memories. It feels like yesterday when I walked into my first AMIMUN conference as a nervous delegate, unsure of my voice and my place. What I found was more than just a platform for debate and diplomacy — I found a home. The people I met, the lessons I learned, and the challenges I faced didn’t just shape my skills; they transformed me. From that wide-eyed delegate to becoming Secretary-General of AMIMUN’24, this journey has been a testament to how kindness, mentorship, and belief can push someone to achieve the extraordinary.

AMIMUN is not just an event; it is a legacy built on dreams, dedication, and a shared vision for a better world. Every individual who becomes a part of this family — be it a delegate, a volunteer, or a leader — contributes a unique thread to this vibrant tapestry. The warmth, generosity, and camaraderie that flow through AMIMUN make it unlike anything else. It’s not just about the sessions, the resolutions, or the awards; it’s about the people — people who inspire you, guide you, and stand by you.

As I take on the role of Chief Advisor for AMIMUN’25, I am deeply moved by the passion and energy that each one of you brings. You are not just participants; you are torchbearers of this beautiful legacy. I encourage you to embrace this experience with all your heart, for it will leave an indelible mark on you, just as it did on me. Here’s to celebrating the spirit of AMIMUN — a home, a family, and a legacy that continues to thrive through each one of us.

With Gratitude,
Gauri Sareen

Sagar Chamarti

Director General, AMIMUN'24

Dear Delegates,

It is my privilege to welcome you to the Amity International Model United Nations Conference 2025. As the Director General of AMIMUN 2024, I have had the profound opportunity to witness the transformative power of diplomatic dialogue and its ability to promote unity across nations.

The theme of AMIMUN 2024, "Optimus ad Optimorum," underscored the importance of striving for excellence and embracing our shared responsibility toward a better world. This legacy now serves as a foundation for the aspirations and initiatives you bring to this year’s conference.

As delegates, you are more than participants; you are visionaries shaping the global narrative. Your discussions and collaborations have the potential to transcend boundaries and foster solutions for a brighter future. With this responsibility comes the incredible power to turn words into meaningful action, and I encourage you to embrace this challenge wholeheartedly.

To the Secretariat and Organizing Committee of AMIMUN 2025, your tireless dedication and collaborative efforts have brought this event to life. From the initial planning stages to the culmination of this remarkable conference, you have shown what true determination can achieve. Your commitment to excellence is an inspiration, and I commend your extraordinary efforts in continuing the AMIMUN legacy.

Delegates, may your debates be enlightening, your resolutions impactful, and your resolve unwavering. Together, let us work toward a world where peace is not just an ideal but a reality realized through our collective will and action.

Warm Regards,
Sagar Chamarti

Jay Yadav

Deputy Director General, AMIMUN'24

Dear AMIMUN Delegates,

Every year, AMIMUN becomes more than just a conference — it becomes a movement of young minds determined to create ripples of change in the world. What sets AMIMUN apart is not only the passion for diplomacy or the pursuit of knowledge, but also the courage of its participants to challenge perspectives, redefine narratives, and imagine a future that is brighter and more inclusive. To be part of this legacy is an honor, and to witness its growth year after year is truly inspiring.

As you step into this journey, I urge you to embrace the power of dialogue, for it is through conversations that divisions are bridged and solutions are born. Diplomacy is not about proving oneself right, but about finding a path where differences transform into opportunities for collaboration. Take every committee session as a chance to learn, to question, and to grow — for these moments will stay with you long after the gavel has fallen.

With every resolution you draft and every perspective you share, remember that you are contributing to something greater — a tradition of unity, hope, and progress. May this conference ignite in you the conviction that the future is not only to be awaited, but to be built by those willing to dream and act.

Warm Regards,
Jai Yadav

Amishi Arora

Dy. Director – Academics & Head, IT, ASET

Dear AMIMUN Family,

The essence of AMIMUN lies not only in its dynamic committees or the brilliance of its delegates, but in the spirit of collaboration and unity it fosters. Each edition reaffirms that diplomacy is not confined to international halls of power — it begins here, with each one of you who dares to take the floor, voice your convictions, and work together toward common ground.

What makes AMIMUN truly remarkable is the sense of belonging it creates — a space where ideas are respected, voices are valued, and differences are celebrated as strengths. It is in this environment that delegates discover not only their ability to lead but also the humility to listen. The friendships you form, the lessons you carry, and the challenges you overcome during this conference will become part of the legacy that AMIMUN continues to build with every passing year.

As future leaders and changemakers, your role in this conference is pivotal. Let AMIMUN 2025 be a reminder that leadership is not about titles, but about responsibility; not about recognition, but about service. Carry forward the lessons, the camaraderie, and the spirit of AMIMUN into your lives, and you will see how this experience shapes not only your present but also the future you create.

With Best Wishes,
Amishi Arora
